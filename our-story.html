Our Story

A Legacy of Flour

"We believe bread is the soul of the table. What began with a single sourdough starter in a home kitchen has grown into a sanctuary for the senses."

2012

The First Sourdough Starter

In a quiet neighborhood kitchen, the Bakerville journey began. Arthur spent six months perfecting a wild yeast starter, nurtured with organic rye and pure spring water. This same starter lives in every loaf we bake today.

2015

The Flagship Opening

We found our home in a historic brick building. Our goal was simple: to create a space where the aroma of fresh baking greeted every neighbor, and craftsmanship was visible in every corner.

2018

Expanding the Craft

Beyond bread, we ventured into artisanal viennoiserie. Hand-laminated croissants and seasonal tarts became our signature, marrying French technique with local ingredients.

2022

A Decade of Community

Celebrating ten years of feeding our community. We renovated our space to include an open workshop, allowing guests to witness the slow, patient process of traditional baking.

Today

The Journey Continues

While we grow, our heart remains small-batch. Every loaf is still shaped by hand, every morning still starts with the same starter, and every guest is still treated like family.
