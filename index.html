Our Story

Flour, Water, Salt, & Time.

Welcome to Bakerville, located in the charming heart of downtown Anoka, Minnesota. What started as a passion project at a farmer's market stall in 2012 has grown into the neighborhood bakery our community gathers around every morning.

We're inspired by the European and Scandinavian baking traditions. Our sourdough starters have been nurtured for over a decade, giving our bread a depth of flavor that only time can create.

We partner with local Minnesota farmers who share our commitment to regenerative agriculture, ensuring that every grain we use is as good for the earth as it is for your table.

"Baking is a conversation between the baker and the dough. We're just here to listen."
