Common Inquiries

Our team provides bespoke delivery and setup within a 20-mile radius. For destinations further afield, we are happy to discuss custom logistics to ensure our products arrive in pristine condition.
Typically, we accommodate gatherings of 25 guests or more for full-service. For more intimate moments, our 'Artisan Boxes' offer the same premium quality in a curated, drop-off format.
Crafting inclusive experiences is at our heart. We specialize in gluten-sensitive sourdough and vegan patisserie options. Please highlight your requirements in your inquiry.
To ensure the highest quality of artisanal preparation, we recommend 4–6 months for weddings and 3 weeks for corporate or private gatherings.
